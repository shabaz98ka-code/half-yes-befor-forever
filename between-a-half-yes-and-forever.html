Begin

Chapter One

⭐

The First Day

Every story begins with a moment

Every story begins with a moment that seems ordinary at first.

It was a Friday — my joining day at school. The classroom was filled with colours, laughter, and unfamiliar faces.

And then I saw you.

Brown top. Black pants.

In a class of forty-four students, you were impossible to ignore.

I didn't know it then… but my story had already started.

Chapter Two

🎒

A Silent Feeling

A boy who fell in love too quickly

You were the most beautiful girl in the class.

And I was just a boy who fell in love too quickly.

You didn't like me much. Sometimes you didn't even want to see my face.

But feelings don't disappear just because they are one-sided.

So I carried mine quietly… inside my heart.

Chapter Three

💭

Loving From Distance

Like breathing

Days turned into years.

I never fully expressed what I felt, but somehow you always knew.

Maybe that's why things felt complicated.

Still, every year my feelings only grew stronger.

Loving you became something natural — like breathing.

Chapter Four

🎭

The Question

Truth and Dare

In 10th standard, during a game of truth and dare, someone asked:

"Do you love anyone?"

I froze.

Fear filled my mind — fear of rejection, fear of heartbreak.

So I made a promise instead.

"I will say the name on the last day of school."

And from that moment, time felt heavier.

Chapter Five

❤️

The Farewell

You wore red

10th March. Our farewell.

You wore red, and somehow the entire room felt brighter.

At the end of the day, my friends reminded me of my promise.

You had already left.

And maybe that gave me the courage.

"I love Fathima."

For the first time, my secret became real.

Chapter Six

📞

The Unexpected Call

Life sometimes surprises us

I went home expecting nothing.

The next morning, my phone rang.

My friend said, "She wants to talk to you."

I didn't believe it.

I thought it was a joke.

But life sometimes surprises us in ways we never imagine.

Chapter Seven

🚲

Returning

Something beautiful was already happening

I returned to school on my bicycle, without expectations.

Everyone looked at me differently.

Everyone smiled.

But I couldn't even look up properly.

"I don't want to create any problems."

And yet… something beautiful was already happening.

Chapter Eight

🌌

The Half Yes

At 9 PM, you said yes

That morning you gave me a half yes.

Later, I didn't even have the courage to message you.

But you messaged me first.

You asked me to propose again.

And at 9 PM, you said yes.

In that moment, time stopped.

Goosebumps covered my skin. Tears filled my eyes.

For the first time, I felt like I was truly someone's special person.

Chapter Nine

🤍

Becoming Us

Best friends who chose each other

After that night, everything changed.

I became more understanding. More patient. More aware of what love truly means.

You often put more effort than me — and I noticed that.

Our relationship became something rare: deep emotional connection, cute teasing, best friends who chose each other.

Chapter Ten

🧙

Our World

Our own universe

We created our own universe.

Imaginary witches. Vampires. Stories only we understand.

At first, I loved you for your beauty.

Today, I love you for your maturity, your understanding, and the way you love me without conditions.

The Final Page

🎂

Happy Birthday,
Fathima

We never made big promises.

Maybe because what we have feels stronger than words.

You didn't just become part of my life — you changed how I see myself.

You made me feel special.

Thank you for choosing me.
Thank you for staying.
❤️ 🌙 ✨

— Always yours, Shabaz
